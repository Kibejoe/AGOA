ABOUT US

The African Growth and Opportunity Act (AGOA) is a U.S. government program aimed at enhancing trade and investment between the U.S. and sub-Saharan Africa.

Supporting Kenyan Exporters to Access the U.S. Market

AGOA provides Kenyan exporters with duty-free access to U.S. markets, helping local businesses expand and succeed globally.

  • Access to the U.S. market without tariffs on eligible goods.
  • Support for sustainable growth and international trade opportunities.
  • Streamlined application process for the AGOA certificate.

By applying for an AGOA certificate, Kenyan businesses can tap into new markets, expand their reach, and boost economic growth.
